The 3E Model
01
Education

Build your foundation

Focus on courses, training programs, and reading materials. Absorb knowledge, learn terminology, and develop a strong theoretical base before moving to practice.

02
Exposure

Turn knowledge into practice

Apply what you've learned through labs, simulations, and shadowing. Build confidence, experiment safely, and identify gaps in your understanding.

03
Experience

Demonstrate mastery

Apply skills across complex scenarios, solve escalation-grade problems, mentor peers, and drive meaningful customer outcomes.
