What changes (light theme tokens only — dark theme untouched)

Surfaces shift from blue-tinted to warm off-white: --bg-page #f4f7fc → #f8f8f7, --bg-page-overlay #eef3f9 → #f2f2f0, --bg-elevated #f0f5fc → #f3f3f1, --bg-card-soft #f1f5f9 → #f4f4f2. Card gradient ends at warm #fcfcfb, sidebar gradient at #f8f8f7. The background mesh glow drops from 22% blue to 5% (faint brand warmth instead of a blue wash). Text moves from slate (blue-gray) to warm stone gray (#1c1917 / #52514d / #76746e) — same contrast, no blue cast. Shadows get warmer and slightly tighter for a crisp, paper-like look. The blue accent itself is unchanged, so brand and dark theme stay as-is.
