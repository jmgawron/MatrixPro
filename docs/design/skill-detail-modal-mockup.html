Skill Details Modal — 3E Expandable Tree (v3)

Left panel uses +/− tree sections driven by Focus area. Change the focus dropdown to see Education-only, Exposure (+ collapsed Education header), or full Experience tree. Collapsed ancestor sections can be manually expanded with +.

  • Education focus → only Education expanded; Exposure & Experience hidden.
  • Exposure focus → Education collapsed header + Exposure expanded; Experience hidden.
  • Experience focus → all three headers; Education & Exposure collapsed; Experience expanded.

Production today still uses cumulative list filtering (v2). This mockup previews the tree enhancement — approve before implementation.
